Keyflow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Keyflow stores game progress such as score, gems, high score, unlocked themes and selected theme locally in your browser.

Keyflow does not intentionally collect personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers or payment information.

Keyflow may include advertising features in the future. Current ad rewards are simulated for testing and do not show real ads yet.

If third-party ads or analytics are added later, this policy may be updated to explain what those services collect.
