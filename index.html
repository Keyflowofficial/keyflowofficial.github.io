About Keyflow

Keyflow is a keyboard-based mini-game arcade with satisfying sound packs, unlockable keyboard shapes, ASMR modes, XP, levels, ad-only gems, local leaderboard and achievements.

How To Play

Games: hit glowing keys, type words, avoid danger keys, clear memory stacks, beat boss keys and tap rhythm keys.

ASMR: use Zen Keys, Squishy Keys and the draggable fidget spinner.

Gems: gems only come from reward ads. Score, XP and achievements come from playing.
