About Keyflow

Keyflow is a keyboard-based mini-game arcade with satisfying sounds, unlockable keyboards, ASMR modes, XP, gems, streaks and achievements.

How To Play

Games: hit glowing keys, type words, avoid danger keys, chase speed bonuses.

ASMR: use Zen Keys, Squishy Keys and the draggable fidget spinner.

Progress: earn XP, level up, unlock achievements, and buy themes.
